Conflict Mediation Solutions (CMS) is a London-based consultancy specialising in boosting the efficiency and impact of Track II diplomacy.

It was founded in 2019 by Malik al-Abdeh, who identified a need for a nimble and forward-thinking consultancy to help larger organisations in the conflict space anticipate and plan for the future. CMS works in partnership with donors, INGOs, think tanks and the private sector to improve understanding of Middle East conflicts, and to develop strategies, initiatives, and networks to achieve meaningful solutions. The CMS team is committed, multi-disciplinary, and builds solutions that are both fresh and effective.

Services

  • Strategy: We give the client the edge by accurately anticipating trends and planning for them well in advance. We identify the ways and means to achieve strategic objectives efficiently and with minimum risk.

  • Stratcomms: Crafting and delivering targeted messaging to a variety of audiences in English and Arabic using sophisticated narratives and effective language.

  • Fundraising: Providing expert advice that helps clients win multi-year funding and tap into strategic partnerships with donors.

  • Evaluation: Conducting methodologically rigorous assessments according to OECD-DAC criteria. Our tailored methods designed to fit Track II and peacebuilding projects help clients use data to inform strategic recommendations.

Differentiators

1. Creative realism

While our approach is creative, and puts a premium on fresh perspectives, our work is anchored firmly to established political and humanitarian realities. We only offer solutions that can be implemented in practice.

2. Rapid delivery

Our tried-and-tested methodologies allow us to deliver results rapidly without compromising quality. Having embraced remote productivity, we address urgent issues promptly and deliver solutions on time.

3. Breadth and depth

We have access to relevant stakeholders from the highest levels of Track I diplomacy to grassroots activists and community leaders. This enables us to connect actors in novel ways and offer a full spectrum of options for our clients.

4. Value for money

We offer quality services at competitive prices, ensuring our clients receive maximum return on their investment. We bill for the job, not the number of days worked. We do not have high overheads and we do not travel business class.

5. Commitment

Better to understand client needs and to deliver on them, we believe in putting in the hours. That’s why we always plan for a sustained engagement and act accordingly from Day One. We’re looking for partnerships that work in the long-run.
